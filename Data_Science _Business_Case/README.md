# POGOH Data Science Business Case

This folder presents an end-to-end data science business case for the POGOH project. It is organized to show how I approach a real business problem from problem framing through data preparation, modeling, analysis, and final recommendations.

## Project Goal

The goal of this project is to demonstrate that I can translate a business question into a practical machine learning workflow and communicate the results in a way that supports decision-making.

This project is designed for reviewers who want to see both the technical process and the business reasoning behind the work.

## What This Project Demonstrates

- Business problem framing and analytical planning
- Data cleaning and preparation workflow
- Exploratory analysis for business insight
- Predictive modeling and model evaluation
- Communication of findings for a non-technical audience
- Final recommendation and business interpretation

## End-to-End Workflow

1. Defined the business context and clarified the analytical objective.
2. Reviewed available data and identified preparation needs.
3. Cleaned and structured the data for analysis and modeling.
4. Explored trends, patterns, and business signals in the data.
5. Built and evaluated machine learning models.
6. Interpreted model results in business terms.
7. Delivered final insights and recommendations through reports.
